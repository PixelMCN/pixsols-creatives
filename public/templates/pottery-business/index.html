How It's Made

Our Process

Every piece goes through a journey of patience, precision, and fire.

01

Sourcing

Natural clay sourced from the riverbanks of Uttar Pradesh. No synthetic additives.

02

Throwing

Hand-thrown on a traditional kick wheel by skilled artisans with decades of experience.

03

Glazing

Natural food-safe glazes applied by hand. Each piece develops unique character.

04

Firing

Kiln-fired at 1200°C. The fire transforms raw clay into durable, beautiful ceramics.
